## Blog Post Template

When writing a new post, use this structure:

```markdown
# [Title]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Project:** P-XX
**Category:** Build Log | Flight Stories | Lessons Learned
**Skills Demonstrated:** [list of IT/aviation skills this post teaches]

## The Problem
What we needed to accomplish and why.

## The Approach
What tools, technologies, and decisions were involved.

## Step by Step
Detailed walkthrough with commands, screenshots, and config snippets.

## What Went Wrong
Honest account of mistakes, debugging, and pivots.
(This is often the most valuable section for readers.)

## The Result
What we ended up with. Screenshots, verification commands, before/after.

## What I Learned
Key takeaways. What would I do differently?

## Try It Yourself
Simplified steps for a reader to replicate this in their own lab.
Minimum hardware requirements. Estimated time.

---
*Built with Claude Code. Lab documented at rpc-cyberflight.com.*
```
