# Flight Training Resources

## Learn to Fly in San Diego

Ron Craighead is an instrument-rated commercial pilot and independent certified flight instructor (CFI) operating from two San Diego airports:

- **Gillespie Field (KSEE)** — El Cajon
- **Montgomery Field (KMYF)** — Kearny Mesa

---

## Why an Independent CFI?

Independent instructors offer advantages over flight school staff instructors:
- **Consistency** — same instructor every lesson (no rotation)
- **Flexibility** — scheduling that works for your life
- **Experience** — instructors who teach because they love it, not to build hours
- **Personalized pace** — training adapted to how you learn

---

## Training Approach

1. **Ground first, air second** — every flight lesson starts with ground preparation
2. **Simulation integration** — practice maneuvers in the sim before the airplane
3. **Multi-format learning** — articles, videos, simulation, and flight instruction
4. **Documentation** — track your progress and build your knowledge base
