## The Learning Philosophy

**"Repetition invokes the law of exercise."**

Learning to fly uses the same principles as learning any complex skill:
- **Read** about it (these articles)
- **Watch** someone do it (video library)
- **Practice** it safely (flight simulator)
- **Do** it for real (flight training)

Each method reinforces the others. A student who has read about stalls, watched a stall recovery video, and practiced stalls in the simulator will handle their first real stall with confidence.
